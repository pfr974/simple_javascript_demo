About this Course

What is Neo Zeon?

Neo Zeon, heralded as the second advent of Zeon, stands at the forefront of the Universal Century's ongoing struggle for autonomy. Neo Zeon represents the zenith of spaceborne aspirations, embodying revolutionary ideologies that have reshaped the socio-political fabric of the Universal Century. It is the vanguard of change, challenging the old guard and catalyzing a new era in human civilization.

What will you learn in this Course?

This course provides a dynamic and in-depth exploration of Neo Zeon as it shapes the zeitgeist of our spacefaring civilization. You will be immersed in the ongoing narrative of Neo Zeon, understanding its roots and witnessing its unfolding influence on the Universal Century.

Our curriculum is designed to navigate you through the core principles of Neo Zeon's ideology, introduce you to the key figures spearheading the movement, and analyze the strategic military operations that are currently defining our era. In-depth discussions will also touch on the cultural and political reverberations of Neo Zeon's actions.

Course Prerequisites

  • A keen interest in Basic Newtype Theory is beneficial.
  • Prior knowledge of the One Year War and Gryps Conflict are required.
